About The Event
Artificial intelligence (AI) has become an inevitable part in our daily life. In this era where AI is gradually making its presence in every aspect of common man's life, computer science researchers are responsible to design and develop AI enabled products that are safe, reliable and ethical.
Through this seminar we try to introduce the notion of developing efficient algorithms as well as the mathematical perspective of AI. Besides this, the seminar attempts to provide an insight about NLP and role of AI in Network Security. The seminar also unveils the upcoming trends in AI research.
This seminar would act as a platform for researchers in AI across India to meet and explore possibilities for collaborative research. We envision to create an opportunity for the students and researchers to interact with eminent resource persons from academia as well as industry.


Venue: College Seminar Hall

Who can attend?
Research Scholars, students and faculties who are interested in the field of AI.
